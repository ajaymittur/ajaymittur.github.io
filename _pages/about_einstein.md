Hi, I’m Ajay, a final year Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Innovation student at Carnegie Mellon University. With experience in developing and optimizing foundational large language models (LLMs) and machine learning infrastructure, most of my work focuses on fine-tuning, inference optimization, and scaling LLMs, with applications in multimodal models, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), agentic flows, and post-training optimization techniques. I am currently working on the [ARC-AGI](https://arcprize.org/arc) challenge with [Prof. Carolyn Rosé](https://cp3a.github.io/) where I'm researching LLM hypothesis generation, test-time learning and inference techniques and much more!

I'm also working with [BNY](https://www.bny.com/corporate/global/en/about-us/technology-innovation/artificial-intelligence.html) to develop a robust, cross-LLM, automatic prompt optimization and evaluation system. The past summer of 2024, I worked at [BILL.com](https://www.bill.com/)'s InfoSec team to develop and integrate threat detection models using ML.

Apart from that, I've also worked at [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/), [Samsung R&D India](https://research.samsung.com/sri-b), [Walmart](https://www.walmart.com), and [Honeywell](https://www.honeywellforge.ai/us/en/about-us/company) in both Machine Learning and Software Engineering, totalling over 2 years of experience.

I also love playing soccer (played it at almost all levels until college), hiking, cycling (mountain biking in particular), and exploring :)
